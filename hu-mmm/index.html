Mental Health In America: The Emotional Toll of Chronic Illness

Managing symptoms of a chronic condition is difficult enough, but emotional andmental challenges can also arise. Often, people aren’t just coping with one health issue, but several, and it can have a negative impact on the mental health of patients and their caregivers.

Beginning in 2017, Health Union launched a cross-platform initiative to prioritize mental health content and conversation across all 17 of its online communities.

Mental Health In America​ aimed to improve understanding of mental health concerns among people with chronic health conditions.

They also sought to bring awareness to mental health challenges, validate concerns and connect audience to support their mental health needs.

Improve Understanding

Health Union conducted large-scale surveys across all of its online communities - people living with various chronic health conditions, such as multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’sdisease, lung cancer and many others. We asked questions to understand mental healthchallenges that may result from life with challenging health problems.
