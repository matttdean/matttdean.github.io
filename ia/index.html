Diagnosis

Bladder cancer symptoms like blood in urine often come and go, so diagnosis can come as quite a shock. Diagnostic tests like cystoscopies and imaging tests can be scary and stressful, but waiting to receive a diagnosis adds to the stress. Bladder cancer symptoms can often be mistaken for things like urinary tract infections or kidney stones.
