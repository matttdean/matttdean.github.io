Legend has it, there once was a world where emptiness reigned, until a magical post office appeared unexplained

Frederick the Fox is stress shedding. Middle aged and miserable, his adult life hasn’t gone the way he dreamed and now his orange fur is all over the floor. But when a package from a passed-on family member falls onto his desk, Frederick is suddenly whisked back to Burrowood, the forgotten town of his favorite childhood memories to run his uncle’s bookstore, The Lost Chapter.

There are only a few problems.

His new business venture is submerged in vines, some of the rambunctious animal residents might actually want to be friends, and a magical post office that was supposed to be a myth just sent him a letter.

What in the wood?
